LITERARY STREETS

We feel that to really get to know North Beach, you have to get it under your feet. Visiting these streets named for authors who lived and worked in San Francisco is a wonderful way to connect to the neighborhood's literary history.

Mark Twain Street
nee Merchant, off Sansome and Washington
Bob Kaufman Alley
nee Harwood Alley, off Filbert and Grant
Jack Micheline Place
nee Pardee Alley, off Grant and Greenwich
Via Ferlinghetti
nee Price Row, off Union and Stockton
Kenneth Rexroth Place
off Columbus and Vallejo
Saroyan Place
nee Adler, off Columbus and Broadway
Jack Kerouac Alley
nee Adler, off Columbus and Broadway
