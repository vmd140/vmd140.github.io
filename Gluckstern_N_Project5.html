LITERARY NORTH BEACH

Welcome to Literary North Beach

a site for adventurous literati

Ground Zero for the short-lived, but highly influential Beat Generation, San Francisco's historic North Beach neighborhood has been a haven for the literarily-inclined since the turn of the 20th century. Browse for books, drink with poets, and check out some of the sites from North Beach's storied past.
